Expressing opinion

I imagine...

Ex: I imagine all of them want their children to be happy and successful.

I think...

Ex: I think setting realistic expectations can be a good thing.

I'm sure...

Ex: I'm sure that everything will work out perfectly in the end.

I guess...

Ex: I guess sometimes you just have to set your own expectations.

If you ask me...

Ex: If you ask me, this is the best movie of the year.

It seems to me...

Ex: It seems to me that we should reconsider our plans before making a final decision.

I suppose...

Ex: I suppose we could try a different approach if this one doesn't work.
