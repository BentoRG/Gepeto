Brazil: Transportation, Population and Regionalization

Transportation in Brazil

Roads / highways

  • Ex JK - to integrate the country
  • Advantages: Cheap to install
  • Disadvantages: lowest load capacity, highest maintenance cost

Railways / trains

  • advantages: high load capacity, low maintenance cost
  • desadvantages: high installation cost
  • used to exell coffee

Waterways / Boats

  • advantages: transporting large loads, usually in containers
  • desadvantages: high installation cost

Airway / airplains

  • advantages: fastest and has a large load capacity
  • desadvantages: expansive

Brazil's population

  • absolute population (populous): Numbers of inhabitants
  • relative population (populated): number of people divided by Km²
  • Birth rate: number of Birth by 1000 inhabitants in 1 year
  • Death rate: number of Deaths by 1000 inhabitants
  • fertility rate: number of kids by a woman between 15-69 year
  • natural growth: difference between birth and death
